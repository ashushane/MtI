Cetaphil
Gentle Skin Cleanser

HOW TO USE

It's suitable for all skin types. It can also be used daily and only apply a small amount on your face, gently massage and then rinse off with water or wipe with a soft cloth.


PRECAUTIONARY

1. Do a patch test on a small area of your skin, especially if you have eczema or other sensitive skin conditions.

2. Avoid getting the product into your eyes. If contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water.

3. Do not use on severely irritated skin unless recommended by a healthcare provider.

4. Follow up with a moisturizer after cleansing to maintain skin hydration in order to avoid skin irritation.

5. Discontinue use if you experience redness, burning, or itching, and seek medical advice if symptoms persist.


DISPOSAL

Rinse the empty bottle and recycle it according to local guidelines, or dispose of it in regular trash if recycling is unavailable.


SUITABLE FOR FOLLOWING SKIN TYPES

Sensitive Skin: It is specifically formulated for sensitive skin, being fragrance-free, non-irritating, and hypoallergenic. Its gentle ingredients help clean without causing redness or discomfort​.

Dry Skin: The cleanser includes moisturizing ingredients like glycerin, which help retain moisture, preventing the skin from feeling tight or dry after use​.

Acne-Prone Skin: It is non-comedogenic, meaning it won't clog pores, making it safe for acne-prone skin. The mild formula ensures that it doesn't exacerbate breakouts​.

Normal to Combination Skin: The gentle yet effective formula works for people with normal to combination skin, balancing moisture without stripping the skin's natural oils​.

Oily Skin: Although it is hydrating, its gentle properties make it usable for oily skin types, but those with extremely oily skin may prefer additional mattifying products.
